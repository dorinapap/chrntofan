Notre Histoire

We are three high school students who grew up in Amboise, Loire Valley, France.

The beautiful fields and calm nature inspired our desire to come together and produce cake which has the power to soothe people's souls.

Morale de l'entreprise

Our bakery, C'est Bon, is founded on the mission to provide all of our costumers with elegant and delectable baked cupcakes which simply melt in your mouth.

C' est Bon stands for Love, Integrity, Passion and most importantly Family.

All of this while offering clients with undeniable prices.
